About Us

We as a team from D.Y.Patil College, Akurdi, Pune have developed this "Turf Booking System". Our mentors have constantly helped us in developing this system.

This application is intended to manage all the activities of Online Turf Booking. Admin is the main user of the website who will manage every activity. This project is developed in Python and the database used here is MYSQL using MVC architecture.

This online platform of Turf booking will help the cricket & Football enthusiastic users to book their choice of available slots online. Users can not only book the turf for their favorite sports but also can view their booking history.
